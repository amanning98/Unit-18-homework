My UX/UI Skills

Prototyping

When I prototype, I focus on creating paths and giving markers to users to ensure that they won’t lose their way.

Sketching Wireframes

The approach I take when sketching wireframes is the same I take when I would create storyboards. I focus on main actions first before implementing sub actions.

Visual Design

My visual design process consists of finding inspiration and using imagination, and combining them to create an understandable interface.

Wireframing

My wireframes usually are a straight from sketch process. When I wireframe, I prioritize infromation hierarchy and providing an appreance for the user that gives them a sense of familiarity.

Color and Typography

Choosing colors and typography go hand in hand, they relate exmaple being; fonts like Lora I feel resonate more with warm colors. So when choosing them, I always make sure they both match the theme of the prototype I’m making.

Design Research

Design research is the most important step for myself. I do love implementing my own creativity, however making sure that the interface makes sense for users take priority, and then I implement my ideas into said reference.

Interactive Design

Interactive designing is my way to keep users who need something to keep their attention and also to show friendliness from the other side. So I tend to always fond ways to make components on the interface interactive, but not overwhelming.

Visual COmmunication

Visual communication is also one of the most important parts of helping the user exploring the interface for the first time. I prioritze allowing the user to peacefully look through the interface and prevent infromation overload, by using layout formations and grouping parts of the hierarchy .

Empathy

Empathy starts from the statement for me. Once I know what the goal of the interface we’re making is, that’s when I put myself in the user’s shoes and ask myself questions and scenarios that even though I face them, others could.

Infromation Gathering

I gather infromation to mainly aid with my hierarhy and to see what users are most interested in regrading what we are prototyping.

User Research

As for user research, I participate in conducting user interviews to see what the users what to see as well as what they want clarification on when exploring an interface. Thi s also helps with my empathy and deducing proper heirarchy.

Miro

The tool I use to organize interview information and creating empathy maps, affinity diagrams, and other staements

Figma

My main tool that I use when creating prototypes and my style guides.

Invision

Main tool for creating mood boards and clickable wireframe sketches.

HTML
